Falconing in Michigan

Falconing is the ancient sport of hunting with a raptor. It is still regulated and practiced around the world today, although in small numbers. There is roughly only 100 liscensed falconers in Michigan.

Falconers are the only people (outside of conservation organizations) allowed to own raptors in Michigan. It is quite the process to get your falconing liscense. It roughly goes as follows:

  1. Research and Educate Yourself
  2. Obtain your rifle hunting permit
  3. Take and pass the Michigan Falconing Exam
  4. Get a General/Master Falconer to become your Sponsor
  5. Get your permit, obtain/craft your falconry equipment, and constuct a mews for your raptor
  6. Capture and train your raptor

Only then will you be able to go hunting with your raptor during Falconing season.

Falconry is an interesting but highly regulated sport, so do your research before deciding if falconry is something you want to do.
